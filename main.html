Food Menu

Tacos

Tacos are a traditional Mexican dish consisting of soft or crispy tortillas filled with a variety of ingredients. Common fillings include seasoned meats like beef, chicken, or pork, as well as beans, vegetables, and cheese. Tacos are often topped with fresh salsa, guacamole, sour cream, lettuce, and cilantro, offering a perfect balance of flavors and textures. They're a versatile and flavorful meal, enjoyed as street food or a main course.

price:₹250

Toast

Toast is a simple yet versatile dish made by browning slices of bread until crisp and golden. It can be enjoyed plain or topped with a variety of ingredients, such as butter, jam, avocado, peanut butter, or eggs. Toast is commonly served as part of breakfast or a light snack, offering a warm, crunchy base for both sweet and savory flavors.

price:₹299

Truffle

Truffle is a rare and prized type of fungi known for its earthy, musky flavor and aroma. Truffles are typically used in small quantities to enhance dishes like pasta, risotto, and gourmet sauces. Available in black and white varieties, they are often shaved over food or infused into oils. Their unique, luxurious taste makes them a highly sought-after delicacy in fine dining.

price:₹300

Sushi Roles

Sushi Rolls are a Japanese delicacy made with vinegared rice, fresh seafood, vegetables, and sometimes fruit, wrapped in seaweed (nori). These bite-sized rolls are often filled with ingredients like raw fish, cucumber, avocado, or crab, and served with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger. Known for their fresh, delicate flavors, sushi rolls offer a balance of textures and tastes in every bite

price:₹400

Pulao

Pulao is a fragrant rice dish made with basmati rice, aromatic spices, and vegetables or meat. Lightly spiced and cooked to perfection, it's a flavorful, aromatic, and satisfying meal, often garnished with fried onions, nuts, and fresh herbs

price:₹699

Biryani

Biryani is a rich, flavorful dish made with basmati rice, tender meat (chicken, lamb, or beef), or vegetables, layered with aromatic spices and herbs. It's slow-cooked to perfection, often garnished with fried onions, mint, and saffron, offering a bold, fragrant, and satisfying meal.

price:₹399
